What is this all about?

In Peace Chess you can not capture pieces. Nevertheless, you can win and loose or you can force a draw whilst most pieces move around freely on the board — just like how we whish it would be in real life.

Maybe you realise after some games that some things should just stay as they are and that Peace Chess is as pointless as the war itself. Either way please help Ukraine!
